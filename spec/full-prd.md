# Glimmer Full PRD & Features (v0.1 MVP)

## Problem
Idle time on phones often turns into draining doomscrolling. Existing 'alternatives' are mostly productive micro-learning or mindfulness apps that feel like additional tasks. There is no beautiful, varied, instantly delightful, user-controlled recreational 'joy scroll' that mixes humor, wonder, cute moments, and actual public domain literature/poetry in one calm, rich experience.

## Unique Value Proposition
Glimmer is the doom-free scroll for free moments: a calm, typography-beautiful, API-mashed feed of positive micro-delights and cultural treasures (jokes, cosmic views, cute animals, facts, weather whimsy, **public domain literature, poetry, short stories**). One button for instant hit; immersive scroll for deeper wandering. Leaves you lighter, curious, or smiling — not drained.

## Target Users
- Primary: Busy adults (professionals, parents, creators) with 2-10 min pockets wanting reliable uplift without guilt or algorithms.
- Secondary: Neurodivergent users seeking predictable positive stimulation and low-overwhelm design; literature lovers wanting bite-sized PD treasures; anyone building positive habits around idle time.

## MVP Scope (v0.1)
**In**:
- Beautiful landing with prominent 'Hit me with something good' button + easy entry to full feed.
- Rich vertical feed with 8+ distinct, visually varied card types (Animal, Joke, Quote, Fact, Cosmic/NASA, Weather Whimsy, Activity Spark, Advice, **Literature & Letters** with PD excerpts).
- Vibe filters (Cozy, Curious, Playful, Whimsical, Inspiring, Literary) + 'Surprise Me' mixed mode.
- Optional location + weather contextual theming or dedicated card.
- **Glimmer Reader** modal: Calm, feature-rich reader for literature excerpts and short works (typography controls, progress, theme).
- Favorites (localStorage), recent history, share (text + image or link).
- PWA installable, responsive, fast, accessible.
- Local-first prefs and cache.

**Out**:
- Auth/accounts (v1+).
- Backend persistence beyond local.
- Payments/freemium (core free; later collections or export).
- Heavy AI generation (focus on curated public sources).
- Algorithmic infinite scroll (user-controlled load more).

## Key User Loops
1. Quick hit: Button → single beautiful delight (or random mix) → share or favorite or done.
2. Immersive: Enter feed → scroll/vibe filter/load more → open Literature card → Glimmer Reader for deeper read → favorite/save to personal commonplace.
3. Habit: Daily or free-moment ritual; revisit recent or favorites.

## Success Signals
- High repeat opens in idle moments.
- Share rate per delight.
- Time spent in reader (positive engagement).
- 'Felt lighter' qualitative feedback.

## Future Hooks (v1+)
- Curated collections / 'Commonplace' personal saves synced (optional account).
- More PD sources, themed daily drops (e.g., 'Poetry of the Day').
- Subtle agentic 'surprise me based on my recent glimmers'.
- Offline cache of recent delights + reader texts.
- Export/share packs.

This is scoped ruthlessly for fast, high-quality MVP while delivering the 'jam-packed' rich experience from day one.
